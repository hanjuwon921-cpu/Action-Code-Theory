## 1. Abstract

This paper proposes a fundamental paradigm shift in cognitive science: **consciousness is not a pre-installed feature of the brain, but an emergent byproduct of physical action and sensory feedback loops.**

We dismantle the vague Freudian concept of the "Unconscious" and replace it with **Undetermined Information-Consciousness (UIC)** — low-resolution data that has been received but not yet prioritized. DNA is reframed not as a "thinking code," but as an **Action Code** whose primary function is to generate mandatory movement.

**Core Thesis:**  
**"Action precedes Awareness. Life does not move because it thinks — it thinks because it moved."**  
(Ambulo, ergo sum)

## 2. New Taxonomy of Consciousness

- **Determined Consciousness (DC):** High-resolution information selected by the brain’s Global Neuronal Workspace for global broadcasting and immediate execution.
- **Undetermined Information-Consciousness (UIC):** Vast background data continuously received by the nervous system but remaining below the ignition threshold. It is not "unconscious" — it is **low-sensitivity consciousness** waiting for prioritization.

## 3. The Genetic Action-Code Hypothesis

**Empirical Case Study: The Chicken Egg Model**

The chicken egg provides an ideal, well-studied empirical foundation for the Action-Code Theory. Its developmental process has been extensively documented in embryology, allowing us to reinterpret existing data through a new lens without requiring new experiments.

**The Process:**

1. **Execution of Genetic Action Codes**  
   DNA does not merely grow the embryo. It actively programs specific movements — heartbeat, twitching, beak movements, and more.

2. **Action-Feedback Loops Inside the Egg**  
   Every movement creates resistance against the shell and amniotic fluid, generating continuous sensory feedback that shapes the nervous system and muscles.

3. **Hatching as an Inevitable Overcoming Action**  
   The critical moment is when the fully formed body breaks out of the shell.  
   This is **not** an action driven by pre-existing consciousness or "will."  
   It is a **mandatory survival behavior** executed by a body that has been completed according to genetic action codes, in order to overcome the physical constraint of the shell.

4. **Explosive Data Acquisition After Hatching**  
   The chick is suddenly exposed to air, gravity, light, sound, and other new stimuli. This triggers a massive influx of sensory data, rapidly building the foundation for consciousness.

**Key Insight:**  
Hatching is not the *result* of consciousness, but a powerful **event that supplies a massive amount of raw data** necessary for consciousness to emerge.  
**It is not that consciousness commands action — rather, action (especially overcoming action) supplies the data from which consciousness arises.**

This mechanism mirrors the process in human fetuses: movement in the womb → birth → rapid accumulation of real-world sensory data.
